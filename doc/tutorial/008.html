12. Conclusion

A Pantheon pipeline provides a way for people to run, explore and understand a set of interconnected ECP technologies.

By digging through the scripts, files and inputs that make up a workflow, one can experiment with different options, learn about how the simulations run, and develop new workflows based on the examples.

The scripts and files are designed to be played with, and run in different ways after the initial installation.

It's as simple as digging in, making changes, and seeing what happens.
